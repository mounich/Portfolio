Self Service Portal

Self Service Portal (SSP) is an Enterprise JEE Web application targeted for Beacon Health Options members. It enables members to view their authorizations, claims, and benefits information. Members can also check their limit, accumulated balance amounts, also allows members to update their demographics information, view their deductible and out-of-pocket amounts and appeals information. SSP also provides Message Center functionality, using which the members can communicate with Beacon Health Options clinicians in a secure manner.
